This program has made it incredibly simple to order from some of your favorite food spots.
To start your order, please click on the start order button from there, please click
on the restaurant that you would like to begin placing your order from. At any point, if you
would like to return to the main screen, you can click on the Hungry Rams logo in the top
right corner of every screen. Once on any restaurant screen, to add an item
to the cart, please click the square button to the left of the food. To choose a different restaurant, click the
return button and select your restaurant. Upon selecting all of your food, press the pay button, sign your
name to confirm the price, and press submit!
